Know Our Host

Dr. Kshama Kullhalli

Dr. Kshama Kullhalli is an accomplished academician, Neuroscience researcher with 5 patents in AIML and more than 100 international published papers and a very renowned cognitive fitness coach with more than 38 years of experience. She has worked as Principal and Dean of Engineering College and has impacted life of 10,000+ students. As a passionate advocate for mental health and wellness, Dr.Kshama Kullhalli has dedicated her career to empowering people to take control of their cognitive fitness and unlock their full potential. With a deep understanding of the science behind cognitive fitness and the factors that impact brain health, she combines practical tools, strategies, and techniques to create personalized training programs that help individuals enhance their cognitive performance, increase their resilience, and improve their overall well-being. Dr. Kshama is a highly motivated speaker, coach, and mentor who has worked with individuals, organizations, and institutions across India and around the world. Her innovative approach to cognitive fitness has helped thousands of people achieve their goals and transform their lives. She is on a mission to educate and inspire 100,000 individuals to prioritize their cognitive health and develop the mindset, habits, and skills necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world.
