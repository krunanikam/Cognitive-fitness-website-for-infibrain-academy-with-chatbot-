Yoga

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

This pose helps improve balance and concentration, which can enhance cognitive focus.

Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

This pose increases blood flow to the brain, which can improve alertness and mental clarity.

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

This pose strengthens the legs and core, promoting stability and grounding, which can help with mental stability and focus.

Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)

This pose calms the mind and reduces stress, which can improve cognitive function.

Corpse Pose (Savasana)

This pose helps relax the body and mind, reducing stress and promoting mental clarity.

Alternate Nostril Breathing (Nadi Shodhana)

This breathing technique balances the brain hemispheres, improving focus and concentration.
