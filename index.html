Our Story

InfiBrain Academy, founded in April 2023 by Dr. Kshama Kulhalli, is a pioneering social organization dedicated to promoting cognitive fitness awareness, particularly among women. Dr. Kulhalli's profound understanding of brain health science drives the organization's mission to empower individuals with practical tools and strategies for enhancing cognitive performance and overall well-being. Through free webinars and personalized training programs, InfiBrain Academy reaches thousands, offering them the means to increase resilience and achieve their goals. Dr. Kulhalli's vision extends to impacting 100,000 lives, inspiring individuals to prioritize cognitive health and thrive in an ever-evolving world.

What is Cognitive Fitness?

Cognitive fitness refers to the brain's ability to function optimally, which includes memory, attention, and problem-solving skills. It involves engaging in activities that challenge and stimulate the brain, such as puzzles, learning new skills, and staying socially active. By maintaining cognitive fitness, individuals can enhance their overall well-being and quality of life.
